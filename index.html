About Webster:

Webster is the official Discord bot used by the r/webdev community.

It's an on-going open-source project maintained by a community of active and passionate developers.

Written with modularity in mind, Webster offers a really easy workflow to get into for contributors and new feature implementations.

Currently, Webster is making up the essential core of the r/webdev Discord server in regards of administration.

This bot is written in Node.js with a MongoDB database backing it.

How to contribute?

Webster is completely open-source and the entire codebase is available to the public on GitHub.

If you are interested in joining the community behind Webster, please join our Discord server, and contact us there!

You can also contact us on Reddit!
